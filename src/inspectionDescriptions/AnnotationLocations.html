This inspections indicated incorrect placement of Stripes annotations within action bean class. Those cases are: 1. @SpringBean annotation is applied to public setter. (Tip: rename method or make it private, or remove annotation to valid method) 2. @Validate annotation has field attribute, but is located outside of @ValidateNestedProperties. (Tip: enclose annotation inside @ValidateNestedProperties or remove field attribute) 3. @Validate annotation hasn't field attribute and located within@ValidateNestedProperties. (Tip: add field attribute to @ValidateNestedProperties) 4. @Validate, @ValidateNestedProperties, @ValidationMethod, @DefaultHandler, @HandlesEvent annotations are applied to invalid class member. (Tip: remove annotations from member or annotate correct element according to Stripes docimentation)
